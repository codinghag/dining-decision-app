# Play Store listing copy

## App name (30 chars max)

Forked — Decide Where to Eat

## Short description (80 chars max)

Swipe with friends to pick a restaurant. Save spots from Instagram & TikTok.

## Full description (4000 chars max)

Forked ends the "where should we eat?" group chat spiral.

Build shared collections of restaurants with your friends, then let everyone
swipe — the group's pick wins. No more forty-message threads that end in
"idk, you choose."

SAVE SPOTS FROM ANYWHERE
• Share a restaurant reel straight from Instagram or TikTok to Forked
• Paste a Google Maps or Instagram link
• Search by name, with nearby places first
• Import your saved posts in bulk from an Instagram/TikTok data export

DECIDE TOGETHER
• Start a decision and everyone swipes: right for "in," left for "pass"
• Live vote tallies and a countdown timer keep it moving
• Add a wildcard — a surprise nearby spot joins the deck
• Confetti when the group lands on a winner, directions one tap away

BUILT FOR GROUPS
• Invite friends with a single link
• See group stats: most-picked spots, who you agree with, decision history
• Everyone's saves in one shared collection

THE DETAILS
• Restaurant photos, ratings, price range, cuisine, and live open/closed hours
• Works on the web too — outforked.com — so friends without the app can join
• Sign in with just your email and a one-time code. No passwords.
• No ads. Your data is never sold.

Where to next? Fork it.
